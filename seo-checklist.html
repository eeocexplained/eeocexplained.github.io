SEO Indexing Booster Checklist

Optimize EEOC Explained for faster Google indexing

1️⃣ Submit Sitemap

  • Submit sitemap.xml in Google Search Console
  • Confirm “Success” or “Submitted successfully” message

2️⃣ Request Indexing for All Pages

  • Homepage: /
  • About Page: /about
  • Checklist Page: /checklist
  • Resources Page: /resources
  • Contact Page: /contact

3️⃣ Add Internal Links

  • Add text-based links in index.html to connect all pages
  • Ensure navigation links are working correctly

4️⃣ Verify Meta Tags & Robots.txt

  • Check for <meta name="robots" content="index, follow">
  • Confirm robots.txt includes “Allow: /” and sitemap link

5️⃣ Build Authority

  • Add your site link to social profiles (LinkedIn, X, YouTube)
  • Post or comment about EEOC Explained on relevant forums

6️⃣ Track & Monitor

  • Check Search Console daily for “Indexed” status
  • Review “Performance → Search Results” for impressions
