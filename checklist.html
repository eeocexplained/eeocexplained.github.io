EEOC Complaint Checklist

Use this to track key steps.

  • Confirm deadlines (usually 180–300 days); employer coverage (most laws: 15+ employees; age: 20+).
  • Gather facts & documents (timeline, emails/texts, witnesses).
  • Start intake online/phone/in person.
  • Review and submit your charge.
  • Watch for mediation, investigation requests, and deadlines.
  • Save everything; note Right-to-Sue timelines if issued.
