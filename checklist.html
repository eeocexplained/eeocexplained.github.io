EEOC Complaint Checklist

Keep track of each stage of your case with this quick overview.

  • Confirm filing deadlines (usually 180–300 days).
  • Gather all supporting documents (emails, texts, witnesses).
  • Submit an intake online or at a field office.
  • Respond promptly to requests for info or mediation offers.
  • Track updates via the Public Portal.
  • Review determination & Right-to-Sue notices carefully.
