EEOC Complaint Checklist

Use this quick checklist to track your progress. Your selections are saved in your browser.

  • Submit your charge online, by mail, or in person at your nearest EEOC office.
  • Include emails, notes, witness names, or documents to strengthen your claim.
  • Watch for EEOC communications and reply promptly to stay on track.
  • If offered, mediation can resolve your case faster without a full investigation.
  • The EEOC may collect documents, conduct interviews, or visit the workplace.
  • You’ll receive results or a Notice of Right to Sue, allowing you to pursue your claim in court.
