About EEOC Explained

EEOC Explained was created to make the Equal Employment Opportunity process easier to understand. Our mission is to empower employees and employers with knowledge about rights, responsibilities, and real-world procedures.

Our Focus

  • Breaking down the EEOC process into plain English
  • Providing verified official resources
  • Helping users prepare for filings, mediation, and investigations
