About EEOC Explained

Independent educational resource to make employment-rights information accessible.

Our mission

We translate complex EEOC procedures into clear, practical guidance so people can act with confidence—whether filing a charge, responding as an employer, or learning their rights.

What we do

  • Plain-language explainers and checklists
  • Curated links to official EEOC resources
  • Templates and tips for each stage of the process

What we don’t do

  • We’re not affiliated with the U.S. EEOC
  • We don’t provide legal advice
  • For legal advice, consult a licensed attorney
